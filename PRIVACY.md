# Privacy Policy — Tiny Dinos

_Last updated: 2026-07-18_

Tiny Dinos is an independent video game made by one person. This policy
covers the **Tiny Dinos posting tools** — the scripts in this repository that
publish our own promotional videos to our own social accounts — and the
**tinydinos** website.

Contact: **charlie.clark215@gmail.com**

## The short version

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data about anyone. The
posting tools operate exclusively on social accounts we own, using
credentials we hold ourselves. There are no users other than us.

## What the posting tools do

The tools in `scripts/social/` upload videos and images that we created, to
social accounts that we own (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). They exist so that
one person can run a posting schedule without doing every upload by hand.

**Data accessed:** only what is required to publish to our own accounts —
an access token identifying our own account, and the account's own id and
username, which we read solely to confirm we are posting to the correct
account before uploading.

**Data stored:** access tokens are stored in local files on the developer's
own machine (`.ig_auth.json`, `.yt_token.json`, and equivalents). They are
excluded from version control, are never transmitted anywhere except to the
issuing platform's own API, and are not shared with any third party.

**Data retention:** tokens are kept only while valid and are replaced on
refresh. They can be revoked at any time from the relevant platform's
settings, which immediately and permanently ends the tools' access.

**No third parties.** We use no analytics, advertising, tracking, data
brokers, or processors in these tools. Nothing is sold. Nothing is shared.

**No data about other people.** The tools do not read follower lists,
messages, comments, or any other person's information. They publish our own
content and read back only our own account identity and our own posts'
public metrics.

## The website

The Tiny Dinos website is a static informational site. It does not require
an account, does not use tracking or advertising cookies, and does not
collect personal information. Our hosting provider may keep standard server
logs (including IP addresses) for security and reliability, as essentially
all web hosts do; we do not use those logs to build profiles of visitors.

## The game

The Tiny Dinos game is played locally on your own device. It has no online
multiplayer, no accounts, and no telemetry. Game progress is saved only to
your own machine. Nothing leaves your device.

## Children

The game and its content are suitable for all ages. Because we collect no
personal information from anyone, we collect none from children.

## Your rights

Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to
disclose, correct, or delete. If you believe otherwise, write to the address
above and we will investigate and respond.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the date at the top changes with it.
